Abbington Farm Trails

Contact Us

Book your half-hour arrival slot and then stay and enjoy the Farm Trail for the rest of the day! The site closes at 5 pm (During winter this may vary).

The farm park has many activities for people to do.

Some of these activities are hayrides, pumpkin picking, and a corn maze.

You can also: - visit the animals and see how they live in their natural habitat - play with farm tools, such as a pitchfork or shovel - pick your own produce from the garden to take home with you!
